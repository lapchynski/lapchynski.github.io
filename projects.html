Advanced Energy Vehicle

The Advanced Energy Vehicle was a project that I completed over the course of an entire semester of ENGR 1182 as part of a team of three students. The objective was to create a "vehicle" that would use propellers to travel along a track with hills and stops to the end to pick up a caboose before travelling back, all while being a quick, energy efficient, and safe as possible. This project involved extensive use of SolidWorks, Arduino C, and the iterative design process.
